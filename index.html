What I Do

Focused solutions to help you organize and elevate your digital presence

Website Revamp & Builder Support

I specialize in revamping and organizing existing websites that feel cluttered, inconsistent, or difficult to navigate. I work across Wix, GoHighLevel, WordPress (Divi), and Shopify, focusing on clarity, structure, and visual consistency—without rebuilding from scratch or losing the heart of the brand.

  • Website layout cleanup and restructuring
  • Page and navigation organization
  • Visual consistency (fonts, spacing, colors, alignment)
  • Content reformatting and simplification
  • Desktop and mobile responsiveness
  • Ongoing website updates and maintenance

Digital Products & eBooks

I design clean, well-structured eBooks and digital resources that turn written content into visually engaging, easy-to-read digital products. My approach emphasizes clarity, flow, and brand alignment, ensuring each asset feels polished and professional.

  • eBook design and layout (PDF format)
  • Structuring long-form content for readability
  • Applying consistent branding (fonts, colors, spacing)
  • Designing guides, workbooks, and downloadable resources
  • Preparing files for website downloads or email delivery
